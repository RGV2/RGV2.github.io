My name is Mohammad Ashhar Mushtaq and I am a software engineer at Accenture since Jan 2019. I am an enthusiast of blockchain technology and the disruptive value of decentralized applications.

Outside of my job, I love taking photos. Nothing makes me happier than being in the middle of nowhere with my mobile phone. I have mostly done, and I mostly do, macro photography. I believe that part of why I enjoy it so much is because it's a time when I can be by myself (or with one or two good friends), far from work and digital distractions. Macro photography requires patience, but waiting for hours for the right time (whether it's burning hot or freezing cold out) brings me a peaceful joy that I cannot get anywhere else.

My future goal is that I would like to keep challenging myself. I have so much to learn, it's intimidating. It's also quite a leap going from taking photos of different shots with no-one else around for miles to trying to get into dense areas. I might never get good at all genre, but it's worth shots!
